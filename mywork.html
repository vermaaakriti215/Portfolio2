The Adventure of Ave is a C++ terminal-based interactive fiction game set in the highlands of Ukhrul. Players take on the role of Ave, a courageous horsewoman cursed by a devil to find him before taking 1000 steps—or be turned into a gargoyle forever. The game combines exploration, inventory management, and decision-making across various locations like the Phangeri Hills, Lai Khullen Lake, Chamu River, and an Abandoned Monastery. It features multiple endings based on the player’s choices, adding replay value and engaging storytelling to a classic text-based format.
